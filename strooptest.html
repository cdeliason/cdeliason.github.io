The premise of Strooptest is that every glyph can be approximated by a different glyph, through rotating and/or flipping it. It is named after the Stroop Test, an experiment that times people as they try to identify the names of colors that are printed in different colors. Strooptest was included in an exhibition at the Marymount California University in San Pedro (2016–17). Its further development is underway.
